Account Deletion Policy

At Asrariya, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the permanent deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to:

  • Your user profile and account settings
  • Saved birth chart and alignment data
  • Istikhara session history
  • Divine Timing check-ins and streaks
  • Quran bookmarks and reading progress
  • All other personalized content and data

How to Delete Your Account

We provide two methods for requesting account deletion:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your account.

  1. Open the Asrariya app on your mobile device
  2. Navigate to: Profile → Privacy & Data → Delete Account
  3. Confirm your password for security verification
  4. Review the deletion confirmation and tap Delete Account

Processing Time: Your account will be deleted immediately upon confirmation.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer to submit a request via email:

  1. Send an email to: zaibaitech@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Asrariya"
  3. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your full name (as registered in the app)
    • Confirmation that you understand deletion is permanent

Processing Time: Email requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Data is Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the following data from our systems:

  • Account Information: Email address, password hash, authentication tokens
  • Profile Data: Name (Arabic and Latin), birth date, birth time, birth location coordinates
  • Spiritual Alignment Data: Zodiac sign, ascendant, planetary alignments, dominant elements
  • Activity History: Istikhara sessions, Divine Timing check-ins, streaks, and guidance history
  • App Preferences: Language settings, notification preferences, theme preferences
  • Saved Content: Quran bookmarks, reading progress, Dhikr counter history

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Personal Data: Deleted immediately (in-app) or within 7 days (email request)
  • Anonymized Analytics: We may retain aggregated, anonymized usage statistics that cannot identify you personally (as permitted under GDPR and CCPA)
  • Legal Requirements: In rare cases, we may retain certain data if required by law (e.g., tax records, fraud prevention)

After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will be automatically signed out of all devices
  • Your profile and data will no longer be accessible
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Asrariya again
  • Your email address can be reused to create a new account (no data will be recovered from the deleted account)
