# Privacy Policy - Asrar Mobile App

**Effective Date**: January 3, 2026  
**Last Updated**: January 3, 2026

## Overview

Asrar ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

## Data Collection

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address (only if you create an account)
- **Profile Information**: Name (Arabic & Latin), date of birth, birth location (optional)
- **Spiritual Data**: Questions for Istikhara, compatibility checks, Divine Timing preferences

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, app version
- **Usage Analytics**: Feature usage, crash reports (via Expo)
- **No Tracking**: We do NOT use third-party tracking or advertising networks

## How We Use Your Data

### Guest Mode (Default)
- **Local Storage Only**: All data stored on your device using AsyncStorage
- **No Transmission**: Your data never leaves your device
- **No Account Required**: Full feature access without registration

### Account Mode (Optional)
- **Cloud Sync**: Profile data synced to Supabase (encrypted in transit)
- **No Email Verification**: Accounts are activated immediately (no email confirmation required)
- **Secure Storage**: Authentication tokens stored using expo-secure-store (encrypted)

### AI Features
- **Groq API**: Istikhara questions sent to Groq for AI interpretation
- **No Personal Data**: We do NOT send your name, DOB, or location to AI
- **Anonymous Requests**: AI receives only your question text

## Data Security

### Encryption
- **In Transit**: All network requests use HTTPS/TLS
- **At Rest**: Tokens encrypted with expo-secure-store
- **Local Data**: Profile stored in device-encrypted AsyncStorage

### Access Control
- **Row Level Security**: Supabase enforces user-level access control
- **No Sharing**: We never sell or share your data with third parties
- **No Advertising**: We do not use your data for advertising purposes

## Your Rights

### Access & Control
- **View Data**: Access your profile anytime in-app
- **Edit Data**: Update profile information anytime
- **Export Data**: Use in-app export feature to download your data
- **Delete Data**: Use in-app account deletion feature or contact us

### Guest Mode
- **Delete Locally**: Uninstall app to delete local data
- **No Cloud Data**: Guest mode data is not stored on our servers

## Third-Party Services

### Supabase (Backend)
- **Purpose**: Authentication, profile sync (account mode only)
- **Location**: US-based servers
- **Privacy Policy**: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Groq (AI)
- **Purpose**: Istikhara question interpretation
- **Data Sent**: Question text only (no personal information)
- **Privacy Policy**: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

### Expo (Development Platform)
- **Purpose**: App updates, crash reports
- **Privacy Policy**: https://expo.dev/privacy

## Children's Privacy

Asrar is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us immediately at the email below.

## Data Retention

### Guest Mode
- Data stored locally until app is uninstalled
- No data stored on our servers

### Account Mode
- Profile data retained while account is active
- Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- Authentication logs retained for 90 days for security purposes

## International Users

Our services are provided from the United States. If you access our app from outside the US, your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US. By using our app, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in-app and on our website. The "Last Updated" date will be revised. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

## GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Request limitation of data processing
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing of your data
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@asrar.app.

## CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

- **Right to Know**: Request disclosure of data collected about you
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: We do NOT sell your personal information
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Exercise your rights without discrimination

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@asrar.app.
