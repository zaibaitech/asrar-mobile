1. Introduction

Asrariya ("we", "our", or "the App") is operated by ZAIBAITECH LTD, a company registered in the United Kingdom.

Asrariya is an educational and reflective mobile application providing religious and cultural content, including prayer time utilities, Qibla direction, and personal reflection tools. The app does not provide medical, financial, legal, or professional advice.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how it is shared, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address for:

  • User authentication
  • Secure login
  • Account recovery
  • Synchronisation of your data across devices

We do not use your email for marketing without your explicit consent.

2.2 Optional Profile Information

The following profile fields are completely optional:

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Place of Birth
  • Mother’s Name

These fields are used solely to personalise in-app content for educational and reflective purposes. They are not used for advertising, behavioural profiling, automated decision-making, or cross-app tracking.

You may edit or delete this information at any time.

2.3 Location Data

With your permission, we access your device’s precise location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction.

  • Used only for app functionality
  • Not used for advertising targeting
  • Not sold to third parties

You may disable location access in your device settings at any time.

2.4 Advertising Identifier (IDFA / GAID)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may access your device’s advertising identifier:

  • IDFA (iOS)
  • GAID (Android)

On iOS, we request permission via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing the IDFA.

  • If you allow tracking, personalised ads may be displayed.
  • If you decline tracking, non-personalised ads will be shown.

You can manage advertising preferences:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads

2.5 Usage Data

We collect anonymised data about app interactions (such as feature usage and session duration) to improve performance and user experience.

2.6 Diagnostics

We collect technical information including crash logs and performance metrics to maintain stability and reliability.

2.7 AI-Assisted Features

When you voluntarily use AI-assisted features, the text you submit may be transmitted to a third-party AI provider (Groq) solely to generate a response.

We do not intentionally transmit your email address or direct personal identifiers with AI requests.

AI providers are not used for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

2.8 Guest Mode

If you use the app without creating an account, personal information is not stored on our servers. Data remains locally on your device.
