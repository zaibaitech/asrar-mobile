Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 3, 2026
Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Asrariya ("we," "our," or "the App"). Asrariya is a mobile application that provides Islamic spiritual guidance through traditional sciences including Ilm al-Huruf (the science of letters), Istikhara (seeking divine guidance), and other spiritual practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Asrariya, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly (Account Mode Only)

When you create an account with Asrariya, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and communication
  • Name: Your full name for personalized spiritual guidance
  • Date of Birth: Used for astrological and spiritual calculations
  • Place of Birth: Location data (city/country) for astronomical calculations
  • Mother's Name: Optional, used in certain spiritual calculations within Islamic tradition

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate location (city-level) for prayer times and Qibla direction (only if you grant permission)
  • Diagnostic Data: Error logs, crash reports, performance metrics

2.3 Guest Mode

If you use Asrariya in Guest Mode (without creating an account):

  • We DO NOT collect any personal information
  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • Your calculations and spiritual guidance remain completely private
  • No data is transmitted to our servers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Spiritual Calculations: Generating personalized guidance based on Islamic traditional sciences
  • Prayer Times: Calculating accurate prayer times based on your location
  • AI-Powered Guidance: Using your profile data to provide contextual spiritual advice (powered by Groq AI)
  • Account Management: Authentication, account recovery, and security
  • Communication: Sending important updates about the app (you can opt-out)
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app features
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service

4. Third-Party Services

Asrariya integrates with the following third-party services:

4.1 Supabase (Backend & Authentication)

  • Purpose: User authentication, data storage, real-time synchronization
  • Data Shared: Email, name, date of birth, place of birth, mother's name
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.2 Groq AI (Istikhara AI Guidance)

  • Purpose: Generating personalized spiritual guidance using AI
  • Data Shared: Your spiritual questions, profile data (name, date of birth), calculation results
  • Privacy Policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/
  • Note: Data is sent to Groq's servers for processing but is not stored by Groq

4.3 Expo (Mobile Framework)

  • Purpose: App distribution, updates, crash reporting
  • Data Shared: Device information, crash logs, app usage analytics
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt)
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access user data, and only when necessary
  • Local Storage: Sensitive spiritual calculations are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Expo SecureStore)

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active
  • After Deletion: Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Guest Mode: Data is deleted when you uninstall the app (stored only on your device)

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 General Rights (All Users)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format (JSON)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7.2 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

7.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Know what personal information is collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information (Note: We do NOT sell your data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

7.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

  • In-App: Go to Profile → Privacy & Data → Delete Account or Export Data
  • Email: Contact us at privacy@asrar.app
  • We will respond to your request within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

Asrariya is intended for users aged 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@asrar.app.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending an email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of Asrariya after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@asrar.app
  • Support Email: support@asrar.app
  • GitHub: https://github.com/zaibaitech/asrar-mobile

11. Islamic Spiritual Context

Important Note: Asrariya provides spiritual guidance based on traditional Islamic sciences. This guidance is for educational and spiritual reflection purposes only. We encourage you to:

  • Consult qualified Islamic scholars for religious rulings (fatwa)
  • Verify spiritual guidance with authentic Islamic sources
  • Use Ilm al-Huruf and Istikhara as supplementary tools, not as replacements for Quranic guidance and Sunnah

12. Data Processing Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when creating an account
  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide our services
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving app features and security

13. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Compliance with GDPR and CCPA requirements
