Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Asrariya ("we", "our", "us", or "the App"), operated by ZAIBAITECH LTD, a company registered in the United Kingdom.

Asrariya is an educational and reflective mobile application providing content rooted in Islamic cultural heritage, including letter-based numerical tools, prayer time utilities, and reflective exercises. All content within the app is intended solely for personal education, cultural exploration, and self-reflection. The app does not provide professional advice of any kind.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using Asrariya, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect and Why

2.1 Account Registration Data

When you create an account, we collect your email address solely to authenticate your identity, enable secure login, and allow account recovery. We do not use your email for marketing without your explicit consent.

2.2 Optional Profile Information

The following profile fields are entirely optional. You may use the app without providing any of them:

  • Name: Used only to personalise the display within the app.
  • Date of Birth: Used only to generate personalised in-app content for educational and reflective purposes. This data is not used to make predictions or provide professional advice.
  • Place of Birth (city/country): Used only for in-app personalisation of educational content and for calculating accurate prayer times. This is not your live device location.
  • Mother's Name: An optional cultural field used only within certain reflective modules of the app. It is never shared with third parties or used for identification.
Important: All optional profile data is used exclusively for personalisation of content within the app, for educational and reflective purposes only. It does not constitute professional, religious, medical, or legal advice of any kind.

2.3 Precise Location (Prayer Times)

With your permission, we access your device's precise location to calculate accurate local prayer times and Qibla direction. Location is only accessed when you actively use the prayer times feature and is never used for advertising targeting. You may deny this permission at any time in your device settings.

2.4 Device Identifier (IDFA) for Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may access your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android) to serve relevant advertisements and measure ad performance. On iOS, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any advertising identifier is accessed.

2.5 Product Interaction and Usage Analytics

We collect anonymised data about how you interact with the app — such as which features are used and how frequently — to understand usage patterns and improve the app's functionality. This data does not identify you personally.

2.6 Crash and Performance Diagnostics

We collect diagnostic data including crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics to maintain the stability and quality of the app. This data is anonymised and used solely for technical improvement purposes.

2.7 Guest Mode

If you use the app without creating an account, we do not collect or transmit any personal data. All information is stored locally on your device only and is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency policy, if you are using Asrariya on an iOS device, we will request your permission before allowing any third-party advertising tracking via your IDFA.

  • If you allow tracking, Google AdMob may use your IDFA to serve personalised ads and measure ad performance across apps and websites.
  • If you decline tracking, AdMob will only serve non-personalised ads and your IDFA will not be used for cross-app tracking.
  • You may change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iPhone.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Supabase (Authentication and Data Storage)

  • Purpose: Secure user authentication, encrypted data storage, and account management.
  • Data Processed: Email address and any optional profile data you choose to save.
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.2 Groq AI (AI-Assisted Features)

  • Purpose: Powering AI-assisted features within the app for educational context generation.
  • Data Processed: Relevant in-app inputs (e.g. questions or profile context) are sent to Groq for processing. Groq does not store this data after processing.
  • Privacy Policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/

4.3 Google AdMob (Advertising)

  • Purpose: Displaying advertisements to support the free availability of the app.
  • Data Processed: Advertising Identifier (IDFA/GAID), device information, ad interaction data (impressions, clicks).
  • Opt-Out of Personalised Ads: https://adssettings.google.com
  • On iOS: Manage via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • On Android: Manage via Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Note: We do not share your name, email address, or any profile data with Google AdMob.

4.4 Expo (App Infrastructure)

  • Purpose: App delivery, over-the-air updates, and crash reporting infrastructure.
  • Data Processed: Device identifiers, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text; they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms via Supabase Auth.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised personnel only, on a need-to-know basis.
  • Sensitive on-device data is stored using Expo SecureStore, which uses the device's native secure storage.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained for as long as your account remains active.
  • After Account Deletion: All personal data associated with your account is permanently and irreversibly deleted within 30 days of your deletion request.
  • Diagnostic Data: Anonymised crash and performance data may be retained for up to 12 months for technical analysis.
  • Guest Mode: No data is retained on our servers. Local device data is removed upon app uninstallation.

7. How to Delete Your Account

You may delete your account and all associated personal data at any time using either of the following methods:

  • In-App: Navigate to Profile → Settings → Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps.
  • By Email: Send a deletion request to csibaka920@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Upon request, all personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This action is irreversible.

8. Your Rights

8.1 Rights Under UK GDPR and UK Data Protection Act 2018

As a UK-based company, we comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR). You have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw any consent you have given at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You may lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at https://ico.org.uk.

8.2 GDPR Rights (EEA Users)

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) have equivalent rights under the EU GDPR. The same rights listed above apply. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at csibaka920@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Legal Basis for Processing (UK/EU GDPR)

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when creating an account and when optional profile data is submitted.
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to provide the services you have requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving app stability, security, and performance.
  • Legal Obligation: Complying with applicable UK and EU law.

10. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers (including Supabase and Groq) may process data outside the United Kingdom or European Economic Area. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the relevant regulatory authorities, in accordance with UK GDPR requirements.

Asrariya is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at csibaka920@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.
